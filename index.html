Freight Frenzy

This picture is extremely low resolution but I did not have a better one. This is Skepna, our robot from this year's FTC season. A majority of our software was focused on improving the driving experience by using a wide variety of driver assist features. This included auto intake stopping, arm commands, and an overdrive mode. All of Skepna's code can be found on our GitHub Respository.
